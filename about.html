Who We Are

Quality Solar Solutions Provider in Cagayan Valley

Founded in 2022, Topshelf Solar Tech & Innovations has been at the forefront of bringing sustainable energy solutions to Northern Luzon.

We are committed to providing world-class, US-made solar products and installation services at the most affordable prices. Our mission is to make clean, renewable energy accessible to every Filipino household and business.

Located in the heart of Tuguegarao City with sattelite branches in Gattaran and Aparri, Cagayan, we serve the entire Cagayan Valley region with premium solar solutions that help families and businesses achieve energy independence.
