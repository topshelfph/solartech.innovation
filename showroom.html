Choose Your Solar System

Off-Grid System

Complete energy independence with battery storage. Perfect for remote areas without grid access.

  • No electricity bills
  • Works anywhere
  • Battery backup included
  • Higher initial cost
Most Popular

Hybrid System

Best of both worlds - use solar, battery backup, and grid power for maximum reliability.

  • Reduced electricity bills
  • Grid backup available
  • Battery for outages
  • Flexible operation

Grid-Tied System

Connected to the utility grid. Sell excess power back and use grid when needed.

  • Lowest initial cost
  • Net metering benefits
  • No power during outages
  • Grid dependent
