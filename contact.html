FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Residential installations typically take 1-2 days, while commercial projects may take 3-5 days depending on the system size.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes! All our products come with manufacturer warranties. Solar panels have 25-year performance warranties, and inverters have 10-year warranties.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! Our systems are designed to be scalable. You can add more panels, batteries, or upgrade your inverter as your needs grow.

What if I need more than 20 meters of wire?

All packages include FREE 20 meters of wire. Additional wire beyond that will be charged at competitive market rates.

Do you offer financing options?

Contact our sales team to discuss flexible payment arrangements and financing options that may be available for your installation.

How much can I save on electricity?

Savings vary based on your system size and usage, but many customers report 50-90% reduction in their electricity bills.
